Our Story

Students in university frequently get bogged down with homework, exams and projects and forget about essential tasks like doing groceries or laundry. DoMyChores offers students a simple service where they can request their laundry to be done in exchange for a small fee. We are solving problems for two different groups of people - students who do not have time for their chores and students with some extra time who want to earn extra cash.
